Our Films

Our movies are made with Stop-Motion, a technique where it gives us the illusion that something is moving even tho it is just a groop of pictures, all together that "create" movement. And now we are about to release Missing Link.

Characters

Our amazing characters are made with silicone mostly. We have a big team, and different groups that have their own area, puppets, scenery, clothes and more. It isn't an easy job but it is definitely worth it.

Who Are We

LAIKA began crafting films in the beautiful cinematic style known as stop-motion animation. LAIKA CEO Travis Knight had honed his stop-motion skills and developed a deep passion for the art form while working as an animator on projects.
