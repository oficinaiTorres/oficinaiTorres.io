Coraline Doll

Straight from the stop-motion adaptation of Neil Gaiman’s spooky, beloved book, this detailed replica brings Coraline’s doll to vivid life. Over 9” tall, it faithfully reproduces the doll from the LAIKA film, from its yarn hair and button eyes to the removable yellow raincoat. Hidden wire armatures allow you to pose her arms, legs, even her raincoat, for a fantastic collectible that fans of all ages will cherish.
